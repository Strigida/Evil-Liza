Butterfleyes:

Animated Music Video

In a post-apocalyptic world,

Humans need to know how desperate times can get without humanity giving up, so they give humanoid robots the task of measuring how much despair one being can endure.

Produced in co-operation with an all student team from Boise State University, and the GIMM department in the College of Innovation and Design at BSU.

Written by the delicate titanium fingers, and cold-fusion powered brain of Evie Wells.

Directed by the intricut, alien mind of Liz Altmiller.

Huge thanks to the amazing team of talented artists we got to work with on this project:
